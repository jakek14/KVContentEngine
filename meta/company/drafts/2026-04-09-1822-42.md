## Post

40% of enterprise applications will embed AI agents by end of 2026.

That's Gartner's forecast. Up from less than 5% a year ago.

The question isn't whether AI agents are coming to your marketing and sales stack. It's whether you're ahead of them or behind them.

Here's what the teams actually getting value from AI agents are doing — and what most aren't:

What works: research synthesis, first-draft personalized outreach, automated reporting, competitive intel summaries. Fast, thorough, tireless work that used to eat hours every week.

What doesn't work: replacing human judgment on strategy, messaging direction, and what actually matters to your buyer. AI agents know what your data says. They don't know what your customer feels. That nuance matters more than people think.

The teams winning with AI agents treat them like sharp junior analysts — high-output, always supervised, never trusted to make the call alone.

The tools are getting smarter. The teams using them well are getting ahead.
