## Post

For the first time ever, Meta just overtook Google in global digital ad revenue.

$243 billion vs $239 billion.

This isn't just a business headline. It's a signal about how B2B and DTC brands need to rethink where they show up — and when.

Google built a machine that captures intent. You show up when someone already knows what they want.

Meta built a machine that shapes intent. You show up before someone knows they want anything.

The mid-funnel — brand awareness, retargeting, consideration — has officially migrated.

Google still owns high-intent, bottom-funnel search. That doesn't change.

But the phase where buyers form preferences, compare options, and build brand familiarity? That's being won on Instagram, Reels, and Facebook feeds.

The smartest brands aren't picking sides. They're building for the full journey.

Save this if you're reviewing your H2 strategy.
