## Post

Customer acquisition costs jumped 40–60% since 2023.

The answer isn't to spend more on ads.

The brands winning right now are getting more from the traffic they already have.

Most companies fixate on the top of the funnel — because that's where the ads are. But if you can't identify who's already landing on your site, you're leaving the most valuable part of your pipeline invisible.

Visitor identification gives you a list of who those people are: name, email, phone, company — without asking them to fill out a form.

That's how you stretch every dollar you've already spent.

We built KnownVisitors to give you that list.
