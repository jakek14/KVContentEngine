## Post

Canva acquired two AI companies in one week: an agentic AI platform and a marketing automation company.

That's not just a product announcement.

It's a signal about where the entire marketing stack is going.

The shift: AI in marketing is moving from "tool that helps you execute" to "system that manages the full campaign lifecycle."

Select the audience. Generate the creative. Allocate the budget. Measure the outcome. Repeat.

Most of that was human work a year ago.

Gartner forecasts 40% of enterprise applications will embed AI agents by end of 2026 — up from less than 5% in 2025.

Marketing software is at the front of that wave.

The marketers who stay relevant through this aren't the ones who know the most tools.

They're the ones who can set direction, evaluate output, and make the judgment calls AI still can't.

Knowing what "good" looks like is the skill that doesn't get automated.
