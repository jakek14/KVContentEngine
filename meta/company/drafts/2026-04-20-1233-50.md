## Post

70% of the buying decision happens before your first conversation.

Buyers research independently now. They visit your site, compare options, form opinions — before anyone at your company knows they exist.

This changes what your website's job actually is.

It's not a brochure. It's not a form-fill machine.

It's the first — and sometimes only — touchpoint in the buyer journey.

The brands winning in B2B and ecommerce aren't just better at ads. They're better at knowing who shows up and who's actually interested — before those buyers disappear into the dark funnel.

Your website traffic already has the answer. The question is whether you can read it.
