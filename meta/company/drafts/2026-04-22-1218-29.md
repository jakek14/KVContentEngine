## Post

Most retargeting budgets are spent on people who already decided no.

Here's the math:

You drive traffic to your site. 98% leave without converting. You build a retargeting audience from everyone who visited.

But "everyone who visited" is mostly people who bounced in under 10 seconds.

They weren't on the fence. They weren't interested. They came, glanced, and left.

Retargeting them costs you the same CPM as retargeting someone who spent 5 minutes on your pricing page — and came back twice.

The brands making retargeting work aren't targeting all visitors.

They're building audiences from high-intent signals: time on site, pages visited, return behavior, engagement depth.

Not just "was on the site." But "showed up for a reason."

That's why knowing who your visitors actually are — not just that they were there — changes the entire retargeting equation.

We built KnownVisitors to surface the visitors worth following up with.

Not everyone. Just the ones who showed up with intent.
