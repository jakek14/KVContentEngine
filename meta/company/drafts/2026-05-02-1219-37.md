## Post

Discovery just moved into AI.

Shopify products are now buyable inside a ChatGPT conversation — no Google search, no website visit required.

That's not a new shopping feature. It's a signal about where buyers are heading.

Every generation of the internet changed the channel where people first encounter a brand. Search was one era. Social was another.

This is the next one.

The brands building presence in AI now are playing ahead of the curve. The ones waiting for proof will be optimizing for a channel that's already losing share.
