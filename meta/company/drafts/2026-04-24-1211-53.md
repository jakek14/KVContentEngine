## Post

New CCPA regulations took effect January 1, 2026.

Stricter cookie consent requirements. Global Privacy Control signal recognition. New data broker restrictions.

If your ad targeting still depends heavily on third-party pixels and cross-site tracking data, the floor has moved — and most brands haven't noticed yet.

This isn't just a compliance story. It's a targeting story.

Every new consent requirement shrinks the addressable audience for cookie-dependent campaigns. Platforms have less signal to work with. Lookalike audiences get less accurate. Attribution gets murkier.

The brands least impacted are the ones who built first-party data strategies before the rules changed.

Website visit behavior. Email engagement. Purchase history. Signals from customers who actively chose to interact with you.

In 2026, first-party data isn't a competitive advantage.

It's the baseline for functioning ad campaigns.
