## Post

Founders who post on LinkedIn and X see 4x more engagement than their company's brand account.

That's not a content insight.

That's a signal about where trust actually forms in 2026.

B2B buyers don't trust brands. They trust people.

They're not reading your company page to decide if you're worth their time. They're watching what your CEO thinks about the problem they're trying to solve.

When the founder builds in public — shares real opinions, shows up consistently, says something worth repeating — the company page doesn't have to work as hard.

The brand follows the person.

Most companies treat the company account as the primary channel and the founder's content as supplementary.

The ones seeing the best results have it exactly backward.

Your best marketing asset isn't your ad budget.

It might be the person who built the thing.
