## Post

Most brands get Creative Fatigue wrong.

They think the fix is running more ads.

It's actually about having a system.

Three things that separate brands beating it from brands buried by it:

— UGC-style hooks. Raw, authentic openings stop the scroll. They outperform polished creative by 41% on CTR. Most brands still default to studio-quality visuals.

— A weekly testing cadence. Not monthly. Meta's Fatigue Indicator counts exposures across your entire Page — not just active campaigns. You need fresh angles coming in consistently, not a seasonal creative refresh.

— A promotion system. Winners move from test to scale within 7 days. Losing ads get cut, not paused. No evergreen creative running for 6+ months.

Creative Fatigue isn't a volume problem.

It's a discipline problem.
