## Post

D2C brands in 2026 are learning the hard way: you can't acquire your way to profit.

CPMs are up. CAC keeps rising. New customer acquisition is more expensive every quarter.

The brands winning right now aren't spending more on ads.

They're getting more from the traffic they already paid for.

That means knowing who came to your site and didn't convert. Following up with the visitors who showed real intent before they disappeared.

The problem: roughly 90% of website visitors leave without filling out a form. No name. No email. No record of their visit. No way to follow up.

Those aren't tire-kickers. They're buyers in research mode.

KnownVisitors identifies those anonymous visitors — turning the traffic you already paid for into contacts you can actually reach.

Traffic is expensive. Make it count.
