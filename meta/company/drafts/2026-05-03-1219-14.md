## Post

48% of marketers are increasing their retargeting budgets this year.

Most of them are missing their best prospects.

Retargeting audiences are built on trackable actions — clicks, form fills, cart adds.

The visitors who came back multiple times, spent real time on your pricing page, compared your product against alternatives, then left without clicking anything?

Not in your audience. No record. No follow-up.

They showed more intent than half the people in your retargeting pool. They just didn't raise their hand.

More retargeting budget on an incomplete audience just scales the gap.

The first fix isn't more spend. It's knowing who was actually on your site.

That's what KnownVisitors is built for — identifying the visitors your pixel missed, so your retargeting reaches the people who actually showed intent.
