## Post

Creator marketing hit $44 billion in 2026.

Average ROI: $5.78 for every dollar spent.

Most brands still treat it as brand awareness budget.

That's the mismatch.

The brands winning on creator content aren't measuring impressions or brand lift. They're tracking direct conversions — which posts actually drive site visits, signups, and purchases.

Creator content outperforms polished brand creative because it reads as authentic. Authenticity drives action, not just awareness.

The shift that matters: stop treating creator partnerships as a PR line item. Run them like a performance channel. Measure them the same way you track paid ads.

The brands making this mental model change are consistently outperforming the ones still running creator campaigns once a quarter for "awareness."
