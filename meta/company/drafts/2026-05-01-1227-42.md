## Post

More than half of all Google searches now end without a click.

Buyers are getting the answer they needed — inside the search result — and moving on.

For B2B brands built on SEO and organic search traffic, this isn't a temporary dip. It's a structural shift that's accelerating with every new AI update Google ships.

The brands adapting fastest aren't mourning the traffic decline.

They're asking a different question: how do we become the source that shows up inside the AI summary?

That's the new version of ranking on page one.

It's not about being found in the results. It's about being cited in the answer.

The companies investing in authoritative, specific, expert content — the kind that gets referenced in AI-generated responses — are building visibility that doesn't depend on a click.

That's the search strategy for 2026.
