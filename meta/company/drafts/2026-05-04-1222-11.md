## Post

82% of your website traffic comes from mobile.

Mobile converts at 1.8%.
Desktop converts at 3.9%.

Most brands are pouring budget into campaigns to drive more traffic — without fixing the experience that traffic lands on.

The average ecommerce checkout has 21 form fields and a 69.8% abandonment rate. Optimized checkouts use 8-12 fields and cut that abandonment almost in half.

Forced account creation alone kills 23% of conversions.

More traffic into a broken funnel isn't growth. It's a more expensive problem.

The highest-ROI move in e-commerce right now isn't a new campaign. It's fixing what breaks the moment a visitor pulls out their phone.

What's one thing you've changed on your mobile experience that actually moved the needle?
