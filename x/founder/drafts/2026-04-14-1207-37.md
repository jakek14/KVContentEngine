## Post

For the first time ever, Meta is on track to surpass Google in digital ad revenue.

$243B vs $239B.

Two companies. One market. More than half of all digital ad spending on the planet.

Here's what actually matters for founders:

The duopoly isn't weakening. It's concentrating. Both platforms raised prices this year. Both are removing human control from targeting. Both have infinite appetite for more of your budget.

The founders building the most durable businesses right now aren't adding to either platform.

They're building infrastructure that makes every paid dollar work harder. And they're building owned audiences that don't get more expensive every year.

When two companies control the game, the long-term edge belongs to the brands that don't have to play entirely by their rules.
