## Post

51% of all code committed to GitHub is now AI-generated.

The moat used to be: can you build it?

Now: can you build the RIGHT thing for the right person?

That's a harder question.

You can outsource execution to AI.
You can't outsource taste.

Founders who struggle with AI tools usually aren't struggling because they can't code.

They don't have a clear enough picture of the problem they're solving.

That clarity has always been the job.
AI just made it more visible.
