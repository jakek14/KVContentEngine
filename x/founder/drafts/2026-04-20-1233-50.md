## Post

Startup funding just shattered every record.

$297 billion in Q1 alone. 2.5x the previous quarter.

And yet: seed deal count dropped 30% year over year.

That's the part founders miss.

The market looks flooded with capital. More money in the ecosystem than at any point in history. But investors are writing fewer checks, not more. Bigger rounds to a smaller set of companies.

If you're pre-seed or early seed, raising in 2026 is harder than it was in 2021 — despite the headline numbers. VC dollars are concentrating at growth stage. Every partner wants traction that would've qualified you for a Series A five years ago.

This is why bootstrapping KnownVisitors made sense.

The window to find a customer is usually shorter than the window to close a check.

Get profitable. Negotiate from strength, not desperation.
