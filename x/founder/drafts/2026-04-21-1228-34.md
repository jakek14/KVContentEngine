## Post

Bloomberg says Meta's lead over Google in ad revenue will be short.

They might be right.

But the more interesting story isn't who holds the top spot next year.

It's why Meta crossed Google at all.

Google built a machine that captures intent. You show up when someone already knows what they want.

Meta built a machine that generates intent. You show up before someone knows they want anything.

The second model has a higher ceiling. And it reaches buyers at a fundamentally different point — earlier, softer, more emotional.

For founders thinking about ad strategy: this isn't about picking a winner. It's about recognizing that the phase where your buyers form preferences has migrated platforms.

Google still owns the bottom of the funnel. But consideration — the messy, unstructured middle — is increasingly living on Instagram and Reels.

Build for both or cede one deliberately. Just don't ignore the shift.
