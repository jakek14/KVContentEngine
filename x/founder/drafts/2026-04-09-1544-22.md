## Post

He's right about the bifurcation.

Some B2B companies are genuinely accelerating right now. Most aren't.

The difference isn't product.

The ones pulling ahead are winning on distribution infrastructure — specifically, how fast they can get from "someone is interested" to a human conversation.

The ones falling behind are optimizing ad creative while their pipeline goes dark.

Most of the intent signal in your market is already visible. It's in your website behavior, your trial logs, your email engagement. Someone visited your pricing page twice this week. Someone opened your sequence three times and never replied.

That's not invisible data. It's unread data.

The companies accelerating found a way to read it — and act on it faster than their competitors.
