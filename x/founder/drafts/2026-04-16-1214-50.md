## Post

SaaS is back.

In 2025, calling yourself a SaaS founder was almost a liability.

"What's your AI angle?" was the first question from every investor, every press mention, every pitch deck template.

2026 is different.

The companies that kept shipping product, cutting churn, and building real retention — without rebranding themselves as "AI-native" just to get a meeting — are suddenly in demand again.

Every hype cycle has a reversion. We're in one.

The label never mattered. The fundamentals always did.
