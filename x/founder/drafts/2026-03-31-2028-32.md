## Post

CAC is up 40-60% since 2023.

The average SaaS startup now waits 15-18 months to break even on a new customer.

Most founders respond by tweaking their ad creative.

That's the wrong lever.

When acquisition gets expensive, the best growth move is making the customers you already have worth more.

Expansion revenue makes up 40% of total new ARR for the median SaaS company right now. The best ones are past 50%.

New logos are not your only path to growth.

They're just the most expensive one.
