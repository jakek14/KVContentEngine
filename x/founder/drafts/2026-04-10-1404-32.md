## Post

Most founders treat content like a nice-to-have.

"I'll post when things slow down."

Things don't slow down.

Here's what the data says:

Companies where the founder is an active creator get 3x more branded search than those with a silent founder.

Not from ads. Not from SEO sprints. From showing up consistently.

And branded search converts differently than paid. People who found you through your thinking already trust you before they hit your pricing page.

The distribution math in 2026 is brutal:

Paid is expensive. Organic is throttled. Cold email reply rates sit below 4%.

The founders pulling ahead aren't outspending anyone. They're building distribution through consistent, honest, useful posting.

It looks slow. It compounds fast.

Every post is a tiny piece of infrastructure that works 24/7 without a media budget.

Most of the best distribution moats I've watched get built weren't built with ad spend.

They were built one post at a time.
