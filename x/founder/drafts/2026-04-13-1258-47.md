## Post

He's right, and I think founders are finally internalizing it.

2025 was the year everyone rewrote their bio to say "AI Founder" because VCs were allergic to anything else.

Most of those pivots didn't build real businesses.

The founders pulling ahead in 2026 aren't the ones who perfectly timed the AI narrative.

They're the ones who stayed boring — picked one specific customer with one specific pain, and built revenue discipline from day one.

Bootstrapping didn't stop working during the funding mania.

It just stopped getting press.

The hype cycle is over. Revenue is back. Build something that earns.
