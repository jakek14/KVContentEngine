## Post

Q1 2026: $297 billion raised. The biggest VC quarter in history.

The headline sounds like great news for founders.

Here's the catch:

$188 billion — 65% of it — went to 4 companies.

OpenAI: $122B. Anthropic: $30B. xAI: $20B. Waymo: $16B.

This single quarter outpaced every full year of VC activity before 2019.

The money is real. It's just concentrated in a way most founders aren't accounting for.

If you're building a frontier AI model, this market is extraordinary.

If you're building a B2B SaaS product that solves a real problem — fewer deals are getting done, check sizes are bigger, and the bar is higher than the headline suggests.

"Biggest VC quarter ever" is only true if you're one of 4 companies.

For everyone else: the era of raise a seed and figure it out is giving way to something more demanding.

Which is probably a good thing, but it's worth being honest about.
