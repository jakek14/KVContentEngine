## Post

TK nailed the identity shift.

Through 2025, every deck had to say "AI-first." VCs would mentally check out if you called yourself a SaaS company before you hit slide three.

But here's what actually happened in that window: the companies that changed their label and not their product built on sand. The ones who just kept building — and let the AI-first crowd exhaust itself on positioning — came out stronger.

The "AI founder" framing was mostly a fundraising strategy. The underlying question never changed:

Are you solving a real, specific problem for someone who will pay you?

SaaS. AI SaaS. Vertical AI. Agentic. The packaging mutates. The business logic doesn't.
