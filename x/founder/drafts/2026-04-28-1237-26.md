## Post

TK nailed it.

"SaaS is dead. Long live SaaS."

Through 2025, every founder felt the pressure to call themselves an AI founder. VCs weren't reading decks without "AI-native" in the header.

The founders who rebranded without changing what they built got warm meetings.

The ones who kept building their product, serving their customers, and compounding — they're in a completely different position today.

The label changed. The fundamentals didn't.

A product that solves a real problem. Customers who renew and pay more over time. Unit economics that make sense.

Those were the job in 2021.
They're the job in 2026.

The word in front of "founder" was always noise.
