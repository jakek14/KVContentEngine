## Post

83% of B2B buyers fully define their requirements before they ever speak to sales.

The demo isn't where they decide.

They decided in Slack threads, review sites, and AI research sessions.

You either influenced that phase — or you didn't.

## Context

Research on the B2B dark funnel shows that 83% of buyers fully define purchase requirements before speaking with sales, and 73% of the overall buying journey happens before a buyer ever contacts a vendor. This reframes where marketing needs to operate — not just in the visible funnel, but during the invisible research phase. Distinct from recent posts about AI search, zero-click, and social shortlisting — this is specifically about the decision-formation phase happening entirely outside your funnel.
