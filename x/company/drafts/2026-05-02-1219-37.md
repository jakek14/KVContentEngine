## Post

Your analytics tell you what happened.

Not who was there.

That gap — between behavior and identity — is where most marketing budgets go to disappear.

## Context

Most marketing teams are making budget and campaign decisions based on aggregate analytics that show traffic patterns but zero identity. The reframe here is that this isn't a data volume problem — it's a structural gap in how analytics was built. Pairs well with KV's value prop without being a direct pitch.
