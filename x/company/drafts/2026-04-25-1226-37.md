## Post

Content is free now.

AI can generate a blog post, a white paper, a video script in minutes. Every brand has infinite content capacity.

Which means attention is more scarce than ever.

But there's a third layer most marketers are still missing: identification.

Getting someone to read your content is hard. When they do — when a real buyer with a real budget stops on your site and spends time — you still walk away with nothing if you can't identify them.

The competitive moat has shifted:

Content → Attention → Identification

The first two are getting commoditized. The third is where pipeline actually gets built.

That's what KnownVisitors gives you: the identification layer.
