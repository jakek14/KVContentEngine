## Post

Meta just added a new fee to your ad bill.

Location surcharge. On top of CPM. On top of the platform tax you already pay.

Google's not cheaper.

Every dollar you spend on paid traffic is getting more expensive — and the traffic still bounces at the same rate it always did.

The brands building durable growth right now aren't the ones spending more.

They're the ones getting more out of what they already paid to get here.
