## Post

For the first time ever, Meta is projected to outspend Google in global digital ad revenue this year.

$243 billion vs. $239 billion.

This didn't happen because of better ad units.

It happened because Meta owns its users' identity.

When third-party cookies started dying, Google's targeting weakened. Meta barely flinched.

Identity-first beats intent-first.

Every time.
