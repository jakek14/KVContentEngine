## Post

Meta just passed Google in ad revenue.

First time in history.

$243B vs $239B.

For 25 years, search owned the top of the media stack.

Social just took it.

Every marketer's channel mix is about to change.

## Context

EMARKETER April 2026 forecasts Meta will overtake Google in total digital ad revenue for the first time ever. Meta's growth rate of 24.1% nearly doubles Google's 11.9%. The shift reflects AI-powered automation (Advantage+), Reels growth, and social commerce outpacing traditional search-intent advertising. A seismic signal for how brands should be thinking about digital ad allocation.
