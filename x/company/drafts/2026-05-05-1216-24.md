## Post

There are 3 types of visitors on your site right now.

Most companies treat them the same.

---

**Researchers** — first visit, still orienting. They want to understand what you do. The job: be clear. Don't over-pitch. Let the concept land.

**Evaluators** — second or third visit. Pricing page, case studies, docs. They're comparing. The job: reduce doubt. Make the "why us" obvious.

**Deciders** — they've seen enough. Looking for a reason to say yes — or a red flag that makes them walk. Every friction point costs you here.

---

You can design for each stage.

You can write content for each intent level.

But without knowing who each visitor is, you're guessing which stage they're in.

Behavior tells you the stage.

Identity tells you who to actually reach.

KnownVisitors gives you both — the behavioral signal and the name behind it.
