## Post

Buyers don't arrive at your website the way they used to.

In 2026, Google's AI Mode answers product questions, compares alternatives, and surfaces pricing — all before a click.

By the time someone lands on your site, they've already done the research.

They're not casually browsing. They're finalizing a decision.

That makes every website visitor more valuable than they've ever been.

Which makes it even more baffling that most brands have zero infrastructure to know who those people are.

Your highest-intent buyers are landing on your site right now.

Most brands see a session in analytics.

That's the gap.

## Context

Google's AI Mode is completing more of the buyer journey inside the search interface — product comparisons, pricing, feature summaries all delivered before a single click. The result: fewer casual browsers reaching websites, but the ones who do are highly qualified decision-makers in active evaluation mode. This changes the value of a website visit dramatically upward — and makes the failure to identify those visitors even more costly.
