## Post

AI search is changing which visitors reach your site.

Fewer clicks are making it through Google's AI Overviews. More of the answer now lives inside the search result — and on May 20, Google Marketing Live will announce the next wave of AI-powered ads accelerating that trend.

The visitors who still click through in 2026?

They passed the AI summary. They saw the overview. They decided to go deeper.

That's the highest-intent traffic in your funnel's history — people who needed more than what AI could give them.

Most companies have no record of who those visitors are. No name. No company. No way to follow up.

The volume is shrinking. The intent is rising. What you do with every single visitor matters more than it ever has.
