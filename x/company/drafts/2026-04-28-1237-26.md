## Post

Shopify just expanded native B2B features to every paid plan.

Company profiles. Custom pricing catalogs. Payment terms. Volume discounts.

All of this used to require Shopify Plus. Now it's available to millions of merchants at no extra cost.

The signal: B2B buyers expect to evaluate options, get pricing, and commit without scheduling a call.

Your website is your B2B sales team now.

The brands that build for that reality will capture the buyers who never raise their hand.
