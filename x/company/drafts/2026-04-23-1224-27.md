## Post

Google just killed Dynamic Search Ads.

Replacing them with AI Max — and September is the forced migration deadline.

If you're running DSA campaigns right now, here's what you need to do before then:

AI Max hands creative generation, audience matching, and final URL decisions to Google's algorithm. Google's own data shows 7% more conversions on average. But you trade granular control for that lift.

Three things to do now:

— Pull your top-performing DSA search term reports and save them. That data won't transfer cleanly.

— Set brand exclusion lists and URL expansion controls before migration. Defaults are aggressive.

— Review your landing pages. AI Max will expand where it sends traffic — pages that weren't built for ads will get traffic anyway.

The brands who prep now will keep what's working.

The ones who wait will let Google relearn from scratch on their budget.
