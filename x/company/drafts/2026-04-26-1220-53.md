## Post

94% of B2B buyers used an AI tool during their last purchase journey.

They arrived at vendor websites later in the process, with shorter shortlists and firmer opinions.

The research phase didn't disappear. It moved somewhere you can't see.

They didn't discover you on your site.

They confirmed — or eliminated — you there.

## Context

New 2026 research shows 94% of B2B buyers used LLMs during their purchase journey. The implication: the research and shortlisting phase has moved into AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini), where there is zero trackable signal for vendors.
