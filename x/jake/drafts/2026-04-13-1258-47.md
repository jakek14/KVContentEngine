## Post

Doing marketing for a small bootstrapped company is completely different from doing it for a funded one.

No agency. No copywriting team. No media buyer on retainer.

You build it yourself, or it doesn't get built.

What AI tools gave us was the ability to run at a pace that used to require a much bigger team — content, design, testing, research.

But the thing AI didn't change:

You still have to pick the right channels before you pour time or money into them.

Big budgets can test everything and see what sticks.

Small budgets have to be right before you spend.

That forced clarity is actually an edge.
