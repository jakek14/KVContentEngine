## Post

Every marketer now has tools to generate content faster than they can use it.

Which means the bottleneck shifted.

It's not output anymore. It's the original insight that makes someone stop mid-scroll.

AI can write the copy. It can't tell you what people actually care about this week.

That only comes from being close enough to your audience to notice things.

The marketers winning right now aren't the ones using more AI. They're the ones using AI to do more with better thinking.

The tools are table stakes now. The edge is still the idea.
