## Post

Meta changed how attribution works in 2026.

Video views now count at 5 seconds, down from 10.

Link clicks no longer include likes or shares on the same post.

Engage-through replaced engaged-view.

Before you kill a campaign because the numbers look off this quarter — check whether you're comparing to benchmarks from before the methodology changed.

The ruler moved. Make sure you know that before you change the campaign.
