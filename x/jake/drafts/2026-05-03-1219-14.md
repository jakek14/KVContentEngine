## Post

Most retargeting campaigns are built on clicks.

Someone lands, clicks something trackable, and your pixel grabs them.

The problem: the most interested visitors often don't click anything.

They come back multiple times. They spend real time on your pricing page. They read your about section. They compare you against something else. Then they leave.

Not because they weren't interested. Because they weren't ready to raise their hand.

They're not in your retargeting pool. You have no record of them. You can't follow up.

Most brands respond by spending more on acquisition — trying to find another version of that visitor. But that visitor already showed up. You just didn't have the signal to catch them.
