## Post

Fresh ideas, keeping up with trends, standing out.

Those are the top 3 creative challenges marketers reported in 2026.

They're real. But I think most teams are solving them wrong.

"Generating fresh ideas" isn't a creativity problem. It's a system problem.

If your process starts with "okay, what do we post today," you'll always feel behind.

The teams that aren't stuck aren't more creative. They have a framework that generates angles from any topic — not just the ones that feel obvious.

Once that system exists, trends and standing out follow. You can't consistently do either if you're reactive by default.
