## Post

Peter nailed this.

Meta's Creative Fatigue Indicator isn't just a warning label.

It's telling you something structural about how Meta scores your account now.

The key detail most people will miss: it counts exposures across your ENTIRE Page, not per campaign. Ads you ran months ago are still contributing to your fatigue score today.

Marketers who'll get hit hardest: anyone running 2-3 evergreen creatives in rotation for months at a time.

Marketers who'll be fine: people who already have a real creative production system — new angles tested regularly, winners rotated in, underperforming ads cut fast.

This feature punishes an inconsistent creative habit. It rewards people who already had a system.

What's your current creative rotation cadence?
