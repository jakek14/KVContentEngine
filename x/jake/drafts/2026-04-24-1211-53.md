## Post

Adam's right — and it goes deeper than the SEO risk.

The problem with "AI content at scale" isn't just that Google penalizes it.

It's the wrong mental model for what marketing is supposed to do.

Marketing isn't a volume game. It's an attention game. And generic content — at any speed, at any scale — doesn't earn attention.

The marketers actually winning with AI right now aren't publishing more. They're using it to understand their audience better, write more precise messages, and test angles faster than anyone could manually.

Volume is what mediocre marketers optimized for before AI. The tools just made that mistake cheaper and faster.
