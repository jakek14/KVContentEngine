## Post

At a small company, "hybrid human-agent" marketing isn't a future state you plan for.

It's already the reality.

Most big teams are "strategically adopting" AI in marketing. Small teams are using it out of necessity.

That urgency is actually the edge. When you can't throw budget at a problem, you figure out the tools faster than anyone.

The companies that get good at this first won't be the biggest ones.
