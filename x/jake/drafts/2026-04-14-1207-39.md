## Post

Companies grow 1.4x faster when their marketing leader is involved in strategic decisions.

Not just campaigns. Strategy.

Only half of CMOs are currently in that room.

On a small bootstrapped team, this problem doesn't exist. There's no CMO separate from strategy. The person running campaigns is the same person deciding what to build, what the positioning is, and what we say to who.

No coordination lag. No "we need to loop in the marketing team" bottleneck. Strategy and execution happen together.

That's not a workaround for not having a CMO.

That's an edge.
