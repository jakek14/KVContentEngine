## Post

TK is right about this.

SaaS didn't die — it was just waiting for the tools to catch up.

Here's what I'd add for anyone building with a small team right now:

The edge isn't just that you can build faster with AI.

It's that every other part of the operation scales too.

Marketing systems, customer support, content — stuff that used to require hiring a whole department, you can now run with one or two people and the right setup.

A small team today can operate the way a much larger team used to.

That's not an exaggeration. That's what it actually looks like on the inside when you're building something real.
