## Post

Research is landing on this and it's not flattering for lazy AI use in creative:

Ads that read as AI-generated underperform on the metrics that actually matter — memory, word of mouth, purchase intent.

People feel the difference. Even when they can't explain it.

This doesn't mean stop using AI in creative. I use it constantly.

It means the job hasn't changed: make something that sticks. AI is a faster brush. You still have to know what you're painting.

The marketers winning in 2026 aren't the ones generating the most creative variations.

They're the ones who can tell which variation is actually good.

That judgment is still the job. It's not getting outsourced.
