## Post

Demand gen and lead gen aren't the same job.

Demand gen: publish without a gate. Build real awareness. Make people trust you before they're ready to buy anything.

Lead gen: capture contact info from people who are already warm.

Most B2B companies have them backwards. They gate everything to collect leads before building any trust. Cold leads. Terrible conversion rates.

The brands doing it right publish freely and let real demand accumulate first.

Then they follow up with the people who showed genuine interest.

The gap: most of that interest is anonymous. Someone spent time on your site, read the piece, came back twice — and bounced without filling anything out. No record of that intent anywhere.

That's why we have KnownVisitors in our marketing stack — catching the intent that never made it to a form.
