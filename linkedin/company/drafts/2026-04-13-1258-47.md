## Post

Meta just shared new data: 65% of advertisers are scaling campaigns through Advantage+.

Companies using it are seeing up to 32% lower CPAs compared to manual campaigns.

That's not a small edge. That's a compounding disadvantage for any brand still running audiences manually.

---

Here's what this means for B2B marketing teams:

The skill of paid social is shifting.

Before, the value was in audience construction — knowing exactly who to target, what lookalike signals to layer, which interest stacks to build. That expertise took years to develop.

Advantage+ handles all of that. The AI optimizes placement, audience selection, bidding, and delivery across Meta's entire inventory.

What it can't do: create the creative that earns attention in the first place.

---

The brands seeing the biggest CPA improvements aren't the ones who configured Advantage+ most precisely.

They're the ones who put real investment into creative quality — because that's the only variable the AI can't own on your behalf.

Advantage+ optimizes the delivery.

Your creative determines what gets delivered.

---

The paid social skills that were valuable three years ago are rapidly becoming table stakes or obsolete.

The skill that's compounding in value: knowing what to say and how to say it to the buyer you're trying to reach.

That part is still entirely human.
