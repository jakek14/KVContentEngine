## Post

The average B2B cold email gets a 3.43% reply rate.

Top personalized campaigns hit 40-50%.

The difference isn't writing skill. It's whether the person already has a reason to care.

---

Cold email is cold because the person on the other end has no context for why you're reaching out. You're guessing at their pain, their timing, their situation.

What changes everything: knowing they already visited your site.

They were there. They landed on your product page, your pricing page, your solutions section. They just didn't fill out a form.

That's not a cold lead. That's a warm prospect who got distracted.

When you follow up with someone who's already been on your site — with a message specific to what they looked at and when — you're not sending cold email. You're continuing a conversation they already started.

---

The brands seeing 30-40%+ response rates aren't better copywriters.

They're working from better data.

First-party intent data from your own site is the cleanest buying signal available. KnownVisitors surfaces it — name, email, company — before visitors disappear.

Know who showed up. Follow up like it.
