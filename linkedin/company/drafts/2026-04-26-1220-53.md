## Post

Sending to your full email list is now actively hurting your business.

Gmail's engagement-based filtering in 2026 is aggressive.

When you send to contacts who haven't opened in 90+ days, it doesn't just fail to reach them.

It signals to inbox providers that your content is low-value — which affects deliverability for your active subscribers too.

Your most engaged customers are collateral damage from the segment you should have cut months ago.

A list isn't an asset unless it's a healthy list.

Clean it quarterly. Or let Gmail do it for you — except their version of "clean" means the spam folder for everyone.
