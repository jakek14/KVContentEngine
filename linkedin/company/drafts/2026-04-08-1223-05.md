## Post

Website visitor identification just ranked as the #1 B2B lead gen channel for 2026.

Not LinkedIn. Not Google Ads. Not cold email.

Website visitor identification.

---

Multiple market research reports this year reached the same conclusion independently.

The reasons aren't hard to understand when you look at what's happened to every other channel:

Third-party cookies are functionally dead for a significant chunk of web traffic. AI Overviews and zero-click search are eating organic traffic. Cold email reply rates average below 4%. LinkedIn organic reach is throttled for most company pages.

Meanwhile, the people visiting your website have already cleared every filter. They found you. They clicked through. They're on your pricing page, your case studies, your solutions section.

That's the highest-intent moment in the entire funnel — and most companies have no infrastructure for it beyond a contact form that 98% of visitors ignore.

---

The market is validating what the data has always shown: a warm visitor is worth more than a cold lead.

The question is why it took this long for the industry to build around it.

What's your current strategy for identifying and converting anonymous website visitors?
