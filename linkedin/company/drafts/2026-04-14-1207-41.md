## Post

Google just eliminated keyword targeting from its newest ad format.

AI Max: give Google a URL and a budget. The algorithm decides who sees your ad, where, and when.

65% of Meta advertisers are already running fully automated Advantage+ campaigns.

Both major platforms are removing human control from targeting. Here's what that means in practice — and what to do about it:

**Invest in your landing page, not just your ad creative.**
When you can't control who arrives, the page has to do the qualifying. A vague landing page wastes every click the algorithm sends. The right visitors should instantly know they're in the right place.

**Build content that earns AI citations.**
Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and ChatGPT are now part of how your buyers research. Getting cited in AI-generated answers is the new first-page ranking. Original, specific, authoritative content wins. Generic content gets bypassed.

**Know who actually shows up.**
The visitors who click through an AI-targeted ad have been pre-qualified by the platform's model. That self-selection makes them worth understanding — by company, role, behavior, and intent. The intelligence in that visitor data is what makes the next conversation worth having.

The ad platforms are automating the top of the funnel.

Your job is to do more with what arrives.
