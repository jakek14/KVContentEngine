## Post

75% of B2B marketers are actively shifting to first-party data strategies in 2026.

Two years ago, that transition felt like a best practice for companies ahead of the curve.

Now it feels like a business necessity.

---

Here's what's driving it:

Cookie deprecation is underway. Privacy regulations across the US and EU are tightening. The attribution and targeting infrastructure that powered a decade of digital advertising is being systematically dismantled.

First-party data — information collected directly from your own audience — is what replaces it.

And your website is where most of that data lives.

The problem: the majority of website visitors are anonymous.

They read your content. They browse your pricing page. They come back twice.

You have no idea who they are.

---

The companies building real competitive advantages from first-party data aren't just growing their email lists.

They're identifying who visits, what they engage with, and when they return — then using that signal to reach buyers already showing intent.

That's exactly what we built KnownVisitors to do: turn your anonymous website traffic into a first-party data asset you actually own.

Research from 2026 shows first-party strategies deliver 4x higher conversion rates than third-party targeting.

The transition is already happening.

The question is whether you're building ahead of it.
