## Post

Shopify just expanded native B2B features to every paid plan.

Company profiles. Custom pricing catalogs. Payment terms. Volume discounts.

This used to require Shopify Plus. Now it's available to millions of merchants at no extra cost.

Merchants using these B2B tools see up to a 33% increase in self-serve orders within six months and 4.1x higher reorder frequency compared to DTC channels.

What this signals for B2B marketing teams:

The bar for B2B buying experiences is no longer set by your industry. It's set by every DTC brand your buyer has ever purchased from.

Buyers expect to evaluate options, understand pricing, and make a decision without scheduling a demo or waiting on a proposal.

Your website is your new B2B salesperson.

The companies adapting their marketing to this reality — sharper messaging, frictionless product pages, self-serve-ready content — will capture the buyers who never raise their hand.

The ones still relying on a rep to hold the buyer's hand through every evaluation will be waiting longer each year.
