## Post

The best B2B companies in 2026 stopped gating their content.

No whitepapers in exchange for email. No "download our guide" pop-ups. Just useful information, published freely, building authority over time.

This is the demand-gen-first playbook — and it works:

→ Ungated content gets dramatically more distribution
→ Buyers arrive to sales conversations already educated
→ The quality of inbound goes up, not just the volume

But there's a hidden cost if you run demand-gen without the right infrastructure.

When everything is ungated, you lose visibility into who's actually engaging. You can see page views. You can see which content is popular. But "who came back three times and spent real time on our pricing page?" — that data disappears.

The brands running demand-gen well in 2026 have closed this gap. They publish everything freely and they identify who shows up to read it.

That's how you get the distribution benefits of ungated content without flying blind on intent.

At KnownVisitors, this is the use case we see most often: companies building category authority through content — and wanting to know which prospects are already engaging before anyone fills out a form.

Demand gen first. Identification second.
