## Post

Short-form video is the highest-ROI content format in marketing right now.

HubSpot's 2026 State of Marketing confirms it ranks first across every major platform.

Most B2B companies still treat it like a B2C trick.

---

The data is consistent: short-form vertical video outperforms every other content type on ROI.

Not because the algorithm rewards novelty.

Because it's the format people are actually consuming — including your buyers, between meetings, in the same feeds as every other brand competing for attention.

B2B audiences didn't stop being humans when they opened LinkedIn.

---

The B2B companies getting traction with video aren't producing elaborate campaigns.

They're doing short takes on their category. Quick walkthroughs. Founder commentary. Direct observations about their market and their customer.

The ones still posting static graphics and article links are getting less reach every quarter as platform algorithms push native video harder.

---

The window to get ahead of this is narrowing.

Every quarter more B2B brands figure this out, the more competitive the format becomes.

Moving now — while most competitors still haven't built a consistent video system — is a real distribution advantage.

The question isn't whether B2B video works. The data is settled.

The question is whether you move before or after everyone else does.
