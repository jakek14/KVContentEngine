## Post

Shipping a product in 2026 takes a weekend.

Growing it still takes years.

---

The same AI tools that let one developer build what used to require a team of five also lowered the barrier for every competitor to do the same thing.

The result: faster product cycles. More alternatives in every category. Shorter windows before someone ships something cheaper.

Speed is no longer the edge.

The edge is distribution. Specifically: the ability to turn anonymous interest into identified, reachable prospects before they move on.

---

Every visitor to your site is a demand signal.

The companies that can capture that signal — name, email, company, intent — before the visitor moves to the next tab have a durable advantage that can't be copied overnight.

Building got commoditized.

Knowing who's paying attention hasn't.
