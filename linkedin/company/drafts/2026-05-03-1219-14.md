## Post

Buyers are now researching your product category inside AI tools before they ever visit your website.

They type a question into ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Mode.

They get a structured answer with 3–5 vendor recommendations.

A shortlist forms.

None of that generates a single trackable signal for your marketing team.

---

This is the AI dark funnel — the portion of the buying journey that happens inside AI assistants, completely invisible to your analytics.

Here's what actually gives you visibility in those AI-generated answers:

**Review presence.** AI tools pull from G2, Capterra, and similar platforms. Thin or outdated reviews mean you don't make the list.

**Authoritative content.** AI retrieval systems favor clear, specific, well-structured content about what your product actually does. Not buzzwords. Specifics — use cases, outcomes, how it works.

**Third-party mentions.** Press coverage, category roundups, and comparison articles are what AI uses to validate a brand's relevance. These are no longer just vanity metrics.

**Consistent brand signal.** Brands that appear across search, review platforms, and social carry more weight than brands with one strong channel and silence everywhere else.

The dark funnel was always there. AI just expanded it.

Build your presence in the places buyers research before they're ready to be found.
