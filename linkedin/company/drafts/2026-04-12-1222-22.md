## Post

The best sales team in 2026 isn't making the pitch.

The product is.

---

Companies with strong product-led motions — free tiers, self-serve onboarding, usage-based conversion — are outperforming sales-heavy models on every efficiency metric that matters right now.

Lower CAC. Faster payback. Higher retention.

Not because they hired better salespeople. Because they built software that demonstrates its own value before asking for a signature.

---

Here's the thing that often gets missed:

In a PLG model, a salesperson's job actually gets easier. You're not convincing anyone of anything. You're converting someone who already experienced the value firsthand.

The deals close faster. The customers stay longer. The expansion happens more naturally — because the product earned it.

---

The companies still running purely sales-led models aren't wrong — some markets require it.

But if the fastest path to belief in your product is using it, and you're gatekeeping that behind a demo call and a 12-slide deck — that's friction you're adding. Not your prospect.

---

The product is the pitch.

Build toward that.
