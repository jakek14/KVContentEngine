## Post

Meta ad costs are up 20% year-over-year. Every brand is paying more to reach the same people.

Most are treating this like a bidding problem. It's not.

---

The real issue is what happens after the click.

A visitor lands on your site. They browse. They leave. You paid $13+ CPM to get them there — and you have no idea who they were.

The brands winning in a high-CPM environment aren't just better at ads. They're better at extracting value from the traffic they already paid for.

---

That means identifying who's on your site — before they fill out a form, or bounce without ever filling one out.

That's exactly what we built KnownVisitors to do. When a visitor lands on your site, you know who they are: name, email, company, phone number. No form required.

The same ad budget. Dramatically less wasted spend.

---

Rising CPMs aren't the problem you need to solve.

Paying for anonymous traffic that disappears forever — that's the problem.
