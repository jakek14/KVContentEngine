## Post

Google AI Mode is cutting organic CTR by 61%.

93% of AI Mode queries end with zero clicks to any website.

That's not a trend. That's a structural shift in how search works.

---

Here's what it means for SaaS founders building content strategies right now:

The goal has changed. You're no longer optimizing for traffic. You're optimizing to be cited inside the AI response.

Brands cited in AI Overviews see 35% higher organic CTR and 91% higher paid CTR on the same queries compared to brands that aren't. The flywheel is real: earn a citation → capture more of the shrinking click pool → build the trust that makes your paid ads more effective too.

---

What earns a citation is different from what earns a ranking.

Rankings rewarded consistent content volume. Citations reward precision — specific data, unique frameworks, original research, definitive answers to specific questions.

Vague thought leadership gets consumed inside the AI response and attributed to no one. Specific, defensible claims with original context become the source.

---

The content brief has fundamentally changed.

Not "write about this topic."

Now it's: "what's the most citable, precise, defensible claim we can make about this topic — and what original data or framework backs it up?"

That's a higher bar.

The founders adjusting their content strategy for this now will have a citation moat in 12 months that's very hard to replicate.
